Our Core Values

Rooted in the principles of the International Co-operative Alliance and driven by our commitment to thrift, fairness, and member empowerment.

Thrift & Savings Culture

Encouraging regular savings and responsible financial habits. We build security by helping members grow their savings and access fair credit, directly supporting our core objective to promote thrift.

ICA: Self-help & Self-responsibility

Fair & Responsible Lending

Loans at reasonable rates for provident and productive purposes. We believe credit should be a tool for upliftment, not burden — transparent, equitable, and accessible.

ICA: Equity & Solidarity

Member Control & Ownership

Members use and control their money for mutual benefit. Every voice matters, and we operate democratically, raising funds through shares, deposits, and responsible instruments.

ICA: Democracy & Equality

Honesty & Transparency

We protect members’ money with uncompromising honesty, openness in operations, and clear communication. Trust is our currency.

ICA: Honesty, Openness, Ethical Values

Digital Future & Innovation

Embracing new technology to modernise services, offer convenience, and bridge our 55-year history with seamless digital experiences. Financially inclusive tech for all.

ICA: Education, Training & Information

Community Solidarity

Serving the La Flora community with an open heart, caring for local needs, and fostering mutual aid. Our success is shared with the neighbourhood we call home.

ICA: Social Responsibility & Caring for Others

Equity & Inclusion

Equal access to financial services regardless of background. We break barriers and ensure every member is treated with fairness and dignity.

ICA: Equality & Equity

Inspired by the International Co-operative Alliance (ICA) — Our values honour the seven cooperative principles: voluntary & open membership, democratic member control, member economic participation, autonomy & independence, education & training, cooperation among cooperatives, and concern for community. La Flora CCU unites ICA ethics with local purpose.

Main Objectives

Encourage thrift and savings and provide loan facilities to its members.

Create a source of credit for provident and productive purposes and provide loans at reasonable rate of interest.

Provide opportunity for members to use and control their money for their mutual benefit.

Raise funds by issuing shares, contracting loans, receiving deposits from members and accept donations from different sources.
